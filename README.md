<h1>Thought Ruler Highlander</h1>

<details open>
  <summary>Rules</summary>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Highlander:</strong> All cards are limited to one. All decks are 60 cards.</li>
    <li><strong>Deck Master:</strong> Start with one monster in the EMZ. You may shuffle one card from hand into the deck to summon/set it.</li>
    <li><strong>Summon Limit:</strong>
      <ul>
        <li>Opponent LP ≥ 4000 → 2 summons max</li>
        <li>Opponent LP &lt; 4000 → 3 summons max</li>
        <li>Opponent LP &lt; 2000 → 4 summons max</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li><strong>Nightfall:</strong> Once per turn, pay 500 LP to flip 1 opponent's card face-down.</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Advanced Rules</summary>
  <h3>1. Deck Construction</h3>
  <ol>
    <li>Only 1 copy of each card.</li>
    <li>Main Deck: 59 cards + 1 Deck Master.</li>
    <li>Extra Deck: 0–15 cards.</li>
    <li>No Side Deck allowed.</li>
    <li>Forbidden cards may not be used.</li>
  </ol>

  <h3>2. Deck Masters</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Must be Normal Summonable monsters (no Extra Deck monsters).</li>
    <li>Starts in the EMZ. Cannot be targeted or activate effects while there.</li>
    <li>Can only leave EMZ by being Normal Summoned/Set (costs 2 summons).</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>3. Summon Limit</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>LP-based scaling limit (2/3/4).</li>
    <li>DM summon from EMZ counts as 2.</li>
    <li>Set monsters don't count, except for DM Set from EMZ (counts as 2).</li>
    <li>Flip summons and tokens do not count.</li>
    <li>Negated summons do not count.</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>4. Nightfall</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Pay 500 LP once per turn to flip an opponent's card face-down.</li>
    <li>Works on monsters, spells, traps (not Link monsters).</li>
    <li>Not an effect or chain; ignores protection.</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Philosophy</summary>
  <p>
  Highlander rewards deep card pool knowledge and creative synergy.
  </p>
  <p>
  Deck Masters ensure both players start with a core card, making games more interactive.
  </p>
  <p>
  Summon Limit slows the game, making LP more meaningful and giving a comeback mechanism.
  </p>
  <p>
  Nightfall helps break through established boards and reintroduces interaction.
  </p>
</details>
